June's Comment

It's been a while since I updated this section, but it shouldn't be a surprise that once again

it's BeatumPopcorn doing fanart of my character considering that 90% of the Fanart Section

is mostly his art, and as usual, he keeps blowing my mind on how good the stuff he makes looks.


He's been into a "Manga Mood" he told me, and he decided to draw Big Whiskey in a very manga style

and it looks absolutely fucking brilliant


Big Whiskey was always supposed to be a depressing/melancholic character since he's supposed to be

a more interesting version of me. He was made to have a very friendly and bright appearance that would

heavily contrast with his rather abrasive and depressive personality and overall mood, and somehow

I think Beatum does a better job at presenting this than I ever could


He mentioned himself that he likes drawing my character exactly due to the fact that Big Whiskey

is very melancholic, he likes how sad he is, and Beatum captures this feeling perfectly


I'm just staring at this, and I'm just amazed how much sentiment he put into this piece

I just look into his face, and the little smile and his beady eyes makes it feel like someone

trying to hide hurt under a fake smile, and I fucking love it

The way he managed to make the messy hair he has feel more natural while still retaining the shape

I usually draw Big Whiskey with, and the sketchy line work, this is all pretty briliant


This is legitimately one of my favorite pieces I've ever received, and since I'm already in a very

sad mood, it fits me perfectly.


I apologize if I couldn't say more, my head's spinning as of writting this, and I need some rest

But don't you worry, there is more to come


But other than that, that's it for now, I'll see you when I close my eyes.
